# REPORTED TIMELINE
> Conservative timeline for external stakeholder reporting.

## PROJECT PHASES

### Phase 1 — Requirements & Setup
**Duration:** 1 week
- Project environment configuration
- Database design and schema planning
- UI design system setup
- Development environment preparation

---

### Phase 2 — Authentication & User Management
**Duration:** 2 weeks
- User registration and login system
- Role-based access control
- Two-factor authentication
- Team and department management
- User profile and session management

---

### Phase 3 — Core Ticketing System
**Duration:** 3 weeks
- Ticket creation, management, and workflow
- Priority and status management
- Custom categories and fields
- File attachments and ticket history
- Bulk operations and ticket templates

---

### Phase 4 — Email Integration
**Duration:** 1.5 weeks
- Inbound email to ticket conversion
- Outbound email notifications
- Email template customization
- Multiple mailbox support

---

### Phase 5 — SLA Management & Automation
**Duration:** 2 weeks
- SLA policy configuration
- Business hours and holiday calendar
- Automated breach detection and alerts
- Workflow automation rule engine

---

### Phase 6 — Reports & Analytics
**Duration:** 2 weeks
- Executive dashboard
- Performance metrics and charts
- Custom report builder
- Export functionality (PDF, Excel, CSV)
- Scheduled automated reports

---

### Phase 7 — Knowledge Base & Self-Service
**Duration:** 1.5 weeks
- Help Center article management
- Public and private article categories
- Search and article suggestions
- Customer feedback on articles

---

### Phase 8 — Customer Satisfaction & Asset Management
**Duration:** 1.5 weeks
- CSAT and NPS survey system
- Asset inventory and lifecycle tracking
- Asset-to-ticket linking

---

### Phase 9 — API, Webhooks & Audit Logs
**Duration:** 1.5 weeks
- REST API development
- Webhook integration system
- Complete audit trail and compliance logs

---

### Phase 10 — Client Portal & Notifications
**Duration:** 1.5 weeks
- Customer-facing portal
- Notification center and preferences
- Client account management

---

### Phase 11 — System Settings & Customization
**Duration:** 1 week
- Branding and white-label configuration
- Security policy settings
- Data retention configuration

---

### Phase 12 — Testing, QA & Deployment
**Duration:** 2 weeks
- Comprehensive system testing
- Bug fixes and performance optimization
- Production deployment and configuration
- User acceptance testing
